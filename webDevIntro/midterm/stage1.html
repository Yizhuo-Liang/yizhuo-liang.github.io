Phase 1 - Harvesting

Harvesting Garlic

In the week of July 27, 2002, they gathered a crew at a farmland at Andes, New York. They harvested 10,000 garlic crops together. Then the garlics are stored and dried in barns, pareparing for the use of credito trading in the fall. The whole process of harvesting was documented.
