Phase 3 - Get Garlic

GET GARLIC, GO WIRELESS

For stage 3, a flatbed truck carrying thousands of bulbs of garlic drove around NYC, stopping at different NYC wireless network nodes. People with virtual garlic credits could approach the truck and exchange the credits for real garlic or the other way around.

At a certain point, the general public was also surrounding the truck and attempting to trade other miscellaneous goods with the people inside. After the people in the truck explained what exactly was happening to the people passing by, they quickly caught on and joined in the trading with the older users in the network, though the newcomers were participating almost exclusively with physical items. Many people could understand the idea behind the project and were eager to join in.
