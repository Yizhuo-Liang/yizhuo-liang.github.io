Phase 2 - Trade for Garlic

Trade for Garlic

In a fictional "After the Crash" scenario, organic garlic is recently ordained as new social currency, serving as "credito" for the global shared network in common exchange. In this garlic credito trading system, the market denominators include digital bytes, bandwidth, domains, URLs, network, system and softwares.

Claim Your Garlic!

By signing on as a member at the Trueque club , one Is awarded 1 garlic credito (valued at 30 virtual garlic) . The member can post digital bytes of own assigned garlic value. The member engages in garlic trading with a credito portfolio.
